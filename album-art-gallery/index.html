Privacy Policy — Album Art Gallery

Last Updated: March 29, 2026
Developer: Yoshi (Life Design Studio)

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

2. Local Storage

The App stores two types of information exclusively on your device via Apple's UserDefaults: your saved album collection and onboarding completion status. This information is never accessible to the developer.

3. Third-Party Services

The App displays album artwork and plays 30-second audio previews via Apple's content delivery network (mzstatic.com). No personal data is shared with third parties.

4. Push Notifications

Notifications are generated entirely on-device. No data is sent to external servers.

5. Analytics & Tracking

The App contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

The developer reserves the right to modify this policy. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date above.
