# Privacy Policy for Album Arts Gallery                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                   
  Last updated: March 29, 2026                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                     
  Album Arts Gallery ("the App") is developed by Yoshi (Life Design). This policy explains how the App handles your information.                      
                                                                                                                                                     
  ## Data Collection                                        
  The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                     
  ## Local Storage                
  The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's standard UserDefaults:                                                
  - Your saved album collection                             
  - Whether you have completed onboarding

  This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                     
  ## Third-Party Services         
  The App displays album artwork and plays 30-second audio previews via Apple's content delivery network (mzstatic.com). No personal information is  
  sent to this service.                                     
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Push Notifications                                                                                                                              
  The App may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of the daily exhibition. Notifications are generated entirely on-device
  and no data is sent to external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.                                                
                                                                                                                                                   
  ## Analytics & Tracking                                                                                                                            
  The App contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.
                                                                                                                                                     
  ## Children's Privacy                                     
  The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

  ## Changes to This Policy
  We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
