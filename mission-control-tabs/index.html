Privacy Policy — Mission Control Tabs

Last Updated: April 5, 2026
Developer: Life Design Studio

1. Overview

Mission Control Tabs is a Chrome browser extension that provides a visual dashboard for managing your open browser tabs. This policy explains what data the Extension accesses and how it is used.

2. Data Access

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions to operate:

PermissionWhy it is needed
tabsRead tab titles, URLs, and favicons to display them in the dashboard
activeTabAccess the currently active tab when capturing a screenshot
tabGroupsRead tab group names and colors for visual grouping
sessionsList recently closed tabs and windows for one-click restoration
sidePanelDisplay the dashboard as a browser side panel
host_permissions: <all_urls>Required by Chrome to call captureVisibleTab for screenshot thumbnails

3. Data Usage

Screenshots (thumbnails) are captured using chrome.tabs.captureVisibleTab and stored only in memory. They are never written to disk or transmitted anywhere, and are discarded when the dashboard is closed.

User preferences (view mode, theme, zoom level, background color) are stored in localStorage on your local device and never leave your browser.

4. What Is Never Collected

  • The Extension does not transmit any data to external servers.
  • The Extension does not log, store, or share your browsing history, URLs, or tab contents.
  • The Extension does not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.
  • The Extension does not use cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

6. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last Updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
